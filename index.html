Biblography

Syed Muzamil Basha (SM Basha) was born in Palamaner (Mandal), Chittoor, India, in 1986. He received the B.E. degree in Computer Science and Information Technology from the SITAMS, Chittoor, Affiliated to JNTU, India, in 2008, M.Tech. in School of Information Technology and Engineering from VIT university in 2011, Vellore and awarded Ph.D. degree in School of computer science and Engineering from the VIT (Deemed by university), Vellore, India.
